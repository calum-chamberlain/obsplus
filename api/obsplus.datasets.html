obsplus.datasets.bingham module¶

The Bingham dataset.

class obsplus.datasets.bingham.Bingham(base_path=None)[source]¶

Bases: obsplus.datasets.dataloader.DataSet

The Bingham Canyon dataset includes waveforms recorded during and after the Manefay Slide, one of the largest anthropogenic landslides ever recorded (https://bit.ly/2bsRsyR).Fortunately, due to close monitoring, no one was injured.

I have personally modified and added some phase picks to the events. Without closer examination, this dataset should be used for testing and demonstration purposes only.

obsplus.datasets.crandall module¶

The Crandall Canyon dataset

class obsplus.datasets.crandall.Crandall(base_path=None)[source]¶

Bases: obsplus.datasets.dataloader.DataSet

Seismic data recorded by regional stations associated with the Crandall Canyon Mine Collapse (goo.gl/2ezyZD). Six mine workers and three rescue workers lost their lives in the disaster.

This dataset is of interest to mine seismology researchers because better understanding of the conditions and sources involved will help prevent future disasters.

I have personally modified and added some phase picks to the events. Without closer examination, this dataset should be used for testing and demonstration purposes only.
